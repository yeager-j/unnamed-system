Every adventurer carries a flask of Prisma — a shimmering white tonic that starts to shift between colors as it grows in potency. No two people can agree on exactly what Prisma tastes like. Some swear it tastes like honey, while others insist it tastes like their favorite childhood meal. The exact origin of Prisma is dependent on the campaign setting. It could be provided by the town's temple of healing, extracted from the environment as a natural resource, or brewed by a corporation that specializes in alchemy. 

## Drinking Prisma

A base-level flask of Prisma holds **2 charges** and heals **`2d8 + 4 HP`** per charge. Drinking from your flask is a Standard Action.

You can also administer a charge of your own Prisma to an ally in the same Zone as a Standard Action. The DM may rule you must use your Move Action to reach the ally. The ally recovers HP as if they had drunk it themselves but the charge is spent from your flask. If the ally is Fallen, the Prisma will only have an effect if it has been upgraded to revive Fallen characters.

Your flask refills, free of cost, at the end of every [[2.5 Resting & Exhaustion#Full Rest|Full Rest]], provided the general area you rest in has Prisma facilities (nearly every settlement does).

## Prismatic Upgrades

A character can pay to upgrade their flask, improving its potency or unlocking new effects. Upgrades come from the setting's source of Prisma, such as the temple cleric, the alchemist's guild, or the keeper of the Prisma well. They are paid for in gold or whatever currency the setting uses. Each upgrade costs more than the last, and a flask with several upgrades may layer multiple effects when you drink from it.

The upgrade tree is divided into five colors, each focused on a different role.

| Color  | Effect                                                                       |
| ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Red    | Increases the amount of HP each charge restores.                             |
| Yellow | Adds additional charges to your flask.                                       |
| Green  | Lets your Prisma cure or ward against [[3.7 Ailments, Technicals, & Saving Throws\|Ailments]].            |
| Blue   | Grants a [[3.8 Battle Conditions\|Battle Condition]] buff alongside healing. |
| Purple | Restores SP in addition to HP.                                               |
