There are a number of Ailments that can affect a character. A character cannot be afflicted with more than one Ailment; the most recent Ailment takes priority. At the end of each of your turns while suffering from an Ailment, make a saving throw. A success cures you of the Ailment. Characters recover from their Ailments after combat ends. The [[Downed]] ailment is an exception to both of these rules, as described below.

Unlike other Ailments, a Downed character recovers at the **start of their very next turn**. Additionally, a character can suffer from both Downed and any other Ailment at the same time.

A character with an Ailment cannot receive a Shift. They also cannot initiate or participate in an All-Out Attack.

## Technicals

When a combatant suffering from an Ailment is hit by a specific damage type, they will suffer a Technical. The consequences are similar to being hit by a Weakness; they are [[Downed]] and take **1.5x** damage. The following table shows Ailment and damage type combinations that lead to Technicals.

| Ailment   | Damage Type                   |
| --------- | ----------------------------- |
| Burn      | Aether, Wind                  |
| Shock     | Aether, Slash, Pierce, Strike |
| Freeze    | Aether, Slash, Pierce, Strike |
| Dizzy     | Aether, Elec                  |
| Sleep     | Any                           |
| Forget    | Psy, Elec                     |
| Confuse   | Psy, Wind                     |
| Fear      | Psy, Ice                      |
| Despair   | Psy, Dark                     |
| Rage      | Psy, Fire                     |
| Brainwash | Psy, Light                    |
