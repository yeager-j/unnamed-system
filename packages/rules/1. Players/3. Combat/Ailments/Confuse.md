---
description: Act randomly during your turn.
aliases:
  - Confused
---
While you are Confused, you cannot take Reactions. You must roll 1d4 at the start of each turn to determine your action.

**`1`**: Randomly select an ally within the same or an adjacent Zone. Use your Move action, if necessary, to Engage them and your Standard Action to make a weapon attack against them.
**`2`**: Randomly select an item you are carrying that has an effect when consumed. Consume it.
**`3`**: Do nothing.
**`4`**: Take your turn normally.
