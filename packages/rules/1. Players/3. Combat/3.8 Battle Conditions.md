During combat, each character has three additional statistics that influence damage and accuracy: **Attack**, **Defense**, and **Hit/Evasion**. These stats can either be neutral, increased, or decreased. Characters begin combat with each of these stats in the neutral state. These changes do not stack constructively; Attack cannot be increased twice, but it can be increased and decreased (becoming neutral). If a stat change would be stacked constructively, the duration is increased instead.

## Charged & Concentrating

A character can also be under the **Charged** or **Concentrating** condition. If a character is Charged, their next Physical attack has its damage multiplied by 2.5. Similarly, if a character is Concentrating, their next Magical attack has its damage multiplied by 2.5. As with Attack, Defense, and Hit/Evasion, these conditions do not stack; you cannot be Charged twice. You can be both Charged and Concentrating, though. Unless stated otherwise by the ability or Skill that grants it, Charged and Concentrating are single-use and are consumed by the very next attack. If the attack misses, Charged and Concentrating are still consumed.
