> [!skill] Rebellion Blade
> A colossal almighty attack targeting a single enemy. Extra damage dealt to Downed enemies.
> 
> SP: **99**; Range: **Engaged**; Damage: **Almighty (Physical)**
> 
> **Attack Roll + St or Ma**
> **`1-10`** `16d6 + St or Ma`
> **`11-19`** `16d10 + St or Ma`
> **`20+`** `16d10 + St or Ma` *Auto-Down*
> 
> **Effect:** Hitting a Downed target (including a target Downed by this Skill) increases damage by 2.5x.
