> [!skill] Shield Arts
> Deals weak physical Strike damage to one enemy. Potentially decreases their Hit/Evasion for 3 turns.
> 
> HP: **15%**; Range: **Engaged**; Damage: **Strike (Physical)**
> 
> **Attack Roll + St**
> **`1-10`** `1d4 + St`
> **`11-19`** `1d8 + St` *Applies [[Sukunda]]*
> **`20+`** `1d8 + St` *Applies [[Sukunda]]*, *Critical*
> 
> **Knight Effect:** Before making your Attack Roll, you can spend 3 Valor to change this Skill's Range to **All Engaged** for this turn.
