> [!skill] Usury
> You wield the most powerful force in the universe — compound interest. You lend your enemies your own vitality at a brutal interest rate, but the inability to repay is fatal.
> 
> ***Payday Loan.*** As a Standard Action, loan 25% of your maximum HP (rounded down) to an enemy you can see that does not already have a loan balance. Reduce your current HP by that amount and grant it to the enemy, whose current HP may exceed its maximum as a result. The enemy takes on a **loan balance** equal to the amount loaned, tracked separately from its HP. Outstanding loans dissolve when combat ends.
> 
> ***Consolidation.*** If an enemy has a loan balance, any damage you would deal to it (except through Showtime!) increases its loan balance instead (your side effects still activate).
> 
> ***APR.*** At the end of an indebted enemy's turn, its loan balance increases by 20%, rounded down (minimum 1).
> 
> ***Bankruptcy.*** If an indebted enemy's current HP is ever equal to or less than its loan balance, it goes bankrupt and drops to 0 HP.
> 
> ***Repayment.*** A loan is repaid only when the indebted enemy deals damage to you. Up to the loan balance, that damage becomes repayment instead of harm: reduce the balance by that amount, reduce the enemy's current HP by the same, and restore that much of your own HP. Any damage beyond the remaining balance strikes you as normal.
> 
> ***Liability.*** If an indebted enemy is reduced to 0 HP by any means other than bankruptcy, its balance passes to another enemy of your choice.
