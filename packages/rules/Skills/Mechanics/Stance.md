> [!skill] Stance
> Your fighting style is represented by four stances: Stanceless, Offensive, Defensive, and Virtuose. When you begin combat, your Stance is set to Stanceless.
> 
> ***Switching Stances.*** You switch Stances in the following circumstances:
> - If you make a Basic Attack, you switch to the Offensive Stance if you are Stanceless. Otherwise, you switch to the Defensive Stance.
> - Some Skills cause you to switch Stances. This happens whether or not the attack hits or does damage.
> - If a Skill causes you to switch to the Stance you are currently in, you become Stanceless.
>   
> When you switch Stances, you gain HP equal to your level.
>   
> ***Stance Effects.*** Your Stances provide you with certain bonuses, which are listed below:
>   - **Stanceless:** No effect.
>   - **Offensive Stance:** Your Attack is increased, but your Defense is decreased.
>   - **Defensive Stance:** Your Defense is increased.
>   - **Virtuose Stance:** Your Hit/Evasion is increased and you are Charged.
