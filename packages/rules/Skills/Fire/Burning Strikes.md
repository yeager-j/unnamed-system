> [!skill] Burning Strike
> Deals weak physical fire damage to one enemy.
> 
> SP: **4**; Range: **Engaged**; Damage: **Fire (Physical)**
> 
> **Attack Roll + St**
> **`1-10`** `1d6 + St`
> **`11-19`** `1d10 + St`
> **`20+`** `1d10 + St` *Burn*
> 
> **Brawler Effect:** If in Offensive Stance, *Burn* becomes *Auto-Burn*. Switch to Defensive Stance.
