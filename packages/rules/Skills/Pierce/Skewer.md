> [!skill] Skewer
> A weak piercing attack targeting a single enemy.
> 
> HP: **5%**; Range: **Engaged**; Damage: **Pierce (Physical)**
> 
> **Attack Roll + St**
> **`1-10`** `1d6 + St`
> **`11-19`** `1d10 + St`
> **`20+`** `1d10 + St` *Critical*
> 
> **Knight Effect:** You can spend 2 Valor to turn *Critical* into *Auto-Critical*. You can choose to spend the Valor after seeing the result of your Attack Roll, but you must do so before the DM determines if the Critical lands.
