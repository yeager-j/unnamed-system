Draft replacement for the Prime Time trigger in [[3.6 Follow-Ups, Shifting, & Showtime!]]. The Shift-chain trigger is retired—chains are already heavily rewarded through escalating AP—and Prime Time moves to a shared party meter. ("Applause" is a working name; rename freely.)

> [!skill] Applause
> The party shares an **Applause** meter, which ranges from 0 to 7 and starts every combat at 0. Each time your side Downs an enemy, the party gains **1 Applause**. Applause past 7 is wasted, and the meter empties when combat ends—the crowd does not carry its enthusiasm to the next show.
>
> ***Prime Time.*** While the meter is full, any party member may call their **Prime Time** in place of a Standard Action, provided they are healthy (no Ailment, not Fallen). Doing so empties the meter to 0.

^ap7pt1

**Design notes (not rules text):**

- Downs are counted per enemy Downed, whatever the source—Weakness, Critical, Technical. Enemies Downing *you* grant nothing.
- Who calls it is the party's decision at the table. If two players want it, that's a good problem; the DM should stay out of it.
- Monsters do not have an Applause meter.
- Seven continues the system's motif (Victories, Virtue ranks, Sparks, Knives).
