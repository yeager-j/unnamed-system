> [!skill] Phantom Tracer
> Deals severe slash damage to one enemy. If any of the enemy's stats are increased (Attack, Defense, Hit/Evasion), your respective stat(s) are increased and the enemy's are set to neutral.
>
> Cost: **Full Prime Time Meter**; Range: **Engaged**; Damage: **12d10 Slash (Physical)**
>
> **Thief Effect (1+ Tells):** The target's previously increased stats are decreased instead of being set to neutral. The DM does not roll for Suspicion.
